The South of Lake Garda

Nest Guesthouse is located in Castelnuovo del Garda, one of Puglia's most picturesque areas, characterized by rolling hills, ancient olive trees, and the iconic trulli houses. This central location makes it the perfect base to explore the region's many treasures.

Within a short drive, you can discover charming towns like Alberobello, a UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its dense concentration of trulli, or Ostuni, the "White City" perched dramatically on a hill with panoramic views of the countryside.

The coast is just 20 minutes away, offering pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters. Inland, you'll find historic sites, nature reserves, and vineyards producing Puglia's renowned wines.
