Our Journey

The story of Nest Guesthouse began in 2015, when Simonetta and Gianpietro discovered an empty house tucked away in the peaceful countryside near Lake Garda. Inspired by its quiet charm and natural beauty, they saw the potential to create something special—a warm and welcoming retreat for travelers from all over the world.

Both having grown up in the southern part of Verona, Simonetta and Gianpietro felt a strong connection to the land and local traditions. They dreamed of combining their love for gardening with their passion for home renovation. Their goal was simple: to create a space that feels like home, filled with nature, comfort, and care, where every guest can truly relax and feel at ease.

After three years of thoughtful restoration and working closely with local craftsmen, Nest Guesthouse opened its doors in 2018. Blending traditional charm with modern comfort, the villa is now a cozy, elegant getaway surrounded by a beautiful garden full of unique plants. It's a place built with heart—and ready to be shared with you.

Our Values

Every aspect of Nest Guesthouse is guided by our commitment to these values

Authenticity

At Nest Guesthouse, we believe in staying true to our roots. Every detail—from the design of the house to the plants in the garden—reflects the local culture and history. We want guests to experience the real charm of the Italian countryside.

Comfort

Your comfort is our priority. We’ve carefully designed every space to feel warm, cozy, and relaxing. Whether you’re here for a short visit or a long stay, we want you to feel at home from the moment you arrive

Connection

We value genuine connections—with nature, with the community, and with our guests. Nest Guesthouse is more than a place to sleep; it’s a space to slow down, share stories, and create lasting memories
